HOW IT WORKS

Download

Download and install the ZAG Sell My Car app for your iPhone below.

Tell us about your car

Launch the app and follow the guided interview to help us learn more about your car. It takes about 15 minutes.

Get a guaranteed offer

We contact dealers and have them submit offers to buy your car immediately. The best offer is sent directly to you.

Complete the transaction

Drive to the winning dealership with your offer certificate and they will pay your on the spot.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • Do you share my contact information with car dealerships?

    Not unless you decide you want to accept their offer. ZAG only shares the vehicle information you provide in order to allow them to make an offer. Once the winning offer has been chosen, you decide whether you want to accept the offer and disclose your contact info.

  • How long do offers take?

    We give dealers 24 hours to evaluate your vehicle and make an offer.

  • Is there any obligation to sell?

    Absolutely not. While we ask that you only use the service if you are genuinely interested in selling your vehicle, receiving offers from dealerships requires no obligation on your behalf.

  • How do I except an offer to sell?

    ZAG will provide you with an offer certificate which you will need to take with you when delivering your vehicle to the winning dealership.

  • How do I get paid?

    The dealership will write you a check when you deliver the vehicle to their lot.

  • Is ZAG Sell My Car available for Android?

    At this time we only support iPhone. There are plans to launch on Android in the future but we don't have a timeline to share.
